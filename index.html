The only man burried on the Moon


Eugene Merle Shoemaker was an American geologist who played a key role in training the Apollo astronauts to explore the Moon in a scientific manner. Unfortunately, his greatest dream to walk on the Moon eluded him, since he was not an astronaut due to minor medical issue. In 1999, his ashes were carried to the Moon.

To this day he remains the only person whose ashes were burried on the Moon.

Message in a bottle


A message in a bottle is a form of communication often used to send distress messages, memorial tributes or deceased loved ones' ashes on a final journey. Through the history, its nature was most often romantic.Though most messages are never discovered, the few that are found make for an interesting story.

In 1995, 10-year-old Josh Baker wrote a note that said, My name is Josh Baker. I'm 10. If you find this, put it on the news. The date is 4/16/95. Josh died in a car accident in 2005, and the note was found in 2006 and delivered to his family.

Symbolism and aestheticism of rings


A ring is a neverending circle,a symbol of eternity. The wedding ring, known as the symbol of the pledge of love, serves to signify the neverending love between the couple.

They are mostly made of metal, but can be made from different materials. Trying to sort them by their styles requires a great deal of time. The list of the styles includes wedding ring, mood ring, engagement ring, coctail ring, class ring and championship ring. Rings worn simply for beauty became established in the eighteenth century. Today, stacking rings is a fashion statement.
